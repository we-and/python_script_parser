ITEM 1- LEARN WITH THOMAS: BE NICE TO YOUR FRIENDS
Open with animation of THOMAS talking to the audience. 
1. THOMAS (ON CAMERA)    10:00:02:00
Hello everyone! Today I learnt a very important lesson about... 
CUT to edited images from the relevant episode. A sequence showing THOMAS meeting DIESEL out on the tracks.
2. THOMAS                  10:00:08:04
Not playing rough and tough. 
CUT to a sequence showing DIESEL talking to the Ducklings.
3. THOMAS                  10:00:11:12
Diesel is often mean to other engines and acts rough and tough to impress his friends.
MIX to a montage of shots showing DIESEL being mean and 'ARRY and BURT laughing.
4. THOMAS                  10:00:18:12
But one day, I found Diesel talking kindly and gently to some fluffy ducklings. 
CUT back to DIESEL and THOMAS talking.
5. THOMAS                  10:00:26:00
Diesel was so embarrassed. He was worried his friends wouldn't like him if they thought he was a big softie...
CUT to DIESEL trying to be nice to the other engines. 
6. THOMAS                  10:00:33:18
So I agreed not to tell anyone about the ducklings, as long as Diesel promised to be kind to the other engines.
MIX to THOMAS taking 'ARRY and BURT to see the ducklings.
7. THOMAS                  10:06:45:20
...But it wasn't long before Diesel was being rough and tough again. So I took 'Arry and Bert to see Diesel's ducklings.. 
CUT to 'ARRY and BERT talking to the Ducklings.
8. THOMAS                  10:00:51:22
'Arry and Bert loved the ducklings and they still liked Diesel even when he was being kind. 
CUT to THOMAS talking to the audience.
9. THOMAS 			     10:01:00:06
Diesel learnt his friends would like him even if he wasn't rough and tough.
